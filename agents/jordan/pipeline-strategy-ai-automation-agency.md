## Cold Outreach Strategy

### Email Template Sequence:

**Email 1: The Problem**
```
Subject: Quick question about [their industry]

Hi [Name],

I help [type of businesses] automate their [specific pain point].

Most companies we talk to are spending [X hours/week] on [manual task] - and it's killing their growth.

We've helped [X] companies automate this and see [specific result] in [timeframe].

Quick 15-min call to see if this could work for you?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Email 2: Social Proof**
```
Subject: [Mutual connection] recommended I reach out

Hi [Name],

[Mutual connection] mentioned you might be dealing with [pain point].

We just helped [similar company] [specific result] - here's the case study: [link]

Worth a 10-min chat?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Email 3: The Value**
```
Subject: Automation Q&A - no pitch

Hi [Name],

Not sure if this is relevant, but I do free 15-min Q&A calls for [industry] owners about automation.

No pitch - just answers to whatever questions you have.

If useful, great. If not, no worries.

Book here if interested: [calendly link]

Best,
[Your name]
```

### LinkedIn Outreach:

- Connection requests with personalized note
- Engagement on their posts first
- DM after 3-5 interactions
- Offer value before selling
