## Key Findings: Top 5 Productivity Mistakes

### 1. LACK OF CLEAR POLICIES & EXPECTATIONS
- **Source:** RescueTime survey of 850+ workers
- **Key Finding:** The biggest barrier is lack of clear policies - especially around availability
- **Impact:** Remote workers feel anxious, uncertain, unproductive without guidelines

### 2. "ALWAYS-ON" CULTURE & WFH GUILT
- **Source:** RescueTime, Buffer State of Remote Work
- **Key Finding:** 45% admit to working more hours remotely
- **Impact:** Workers feel pressured to be online constantly, answer every message

### 3. FAILURE TO UNPLUG / NO BOUNDARIES
- **Source:** Buffer 2021 Survey, McKinsey
- **Key Finding:** Biggest struggle is not being able to unplug
- **Impact:** Work-life blend causes burnout (49% report symptoms)

### 4. POOR COMMUNICATION & STATUS UPDATES
- **Source:** RescueTime
- **Key Finding:** Only 10% of teams communicate status throughout the day
- **Impact:** No visibility into who is available, causing anxiety and over-communication

### 5. FEWER QUALITY BREAKS / OVERWORKING
- **Source:** DeskTime, Apollo Technical
- **Key Finding:** Most productive people work 112 min then break 26 min (vs 52/17 pre-pandemic)
- **Impact:** 3-4 breaks per 8-hour day isn't enough; leads to burnout

## Expert Recommendations:

1. **Set clear expectations** around availability and response times
2. **Create communication runbooks** with status update protocols  
3. **Trust employees** to do their work
4. **Schedule breaks** into the day
5. **Have dedicated workspaces** at home
6. **Set "unplug" boundaries** - stop working at specific times
