## 🐦 Twitter/X Thread

**Tweet 1:**
Remote teams aren't less productive.

They're just making 5 preventable mistakes.

A thread 🧵👇

**Tweet 2:**
Mistake #1: No clear policies

When no one knows when to be available, everyone defaults to "always on."

Fix: Define core hours AND protected deep-work time.

**Tweet 3:**
Mistake #2: "Always-on" culture

45% of remote workers admit to working MORE hours.

That's not productivity. That's burnout waiting to happen.

Fix: Measure output, not hours.

**Tweet 4:**
Mistake #3: No boundaries

Your home is now your office 24/7.

Fix: Create a "start" and "end" ritual. Walk to "work." Log off visibly.

**Tweet 5:**
Mistake #4: Poor communication

Only 10% of teams communicate status throughout the day.

Fix: Use AFK (away from keyboard) status intentionally.

**Tweet 6:**
Mistake #5: Not enough breaks

Remote workers now do 112-minute work sprints.

Pre-pandemic? 52 minutes.

Fix: 52/17 method. Batch meetings. Protect focus time.

**Tweet 7:**
The fix isn't a new app.

It's intentional design.

Pick ONE mistake to fix this week.
